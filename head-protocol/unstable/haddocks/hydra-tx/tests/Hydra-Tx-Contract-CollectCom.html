MutateCommitToInitial

Ensures collectCom cannot collect from an initial UTxO.

MutateNumberOfParties

Every party should have committed and been taken into account for the collectCom transaction to be valid. Here we increase the number of parties in input and output but keep the commits unchanged. This simulates the situation where one participant would not have committed already or whose commit would have been ignored by the collectCom transaction.
